Twisted Joy

_Twisted Joy_ is an abstract video that takes a simple balloon and all the actions that typically come with it to create a series of emotions. This is an item that was created for festivities and celebration. Unfortunately, they also create an uncontrollable amount of anxiety because of any motion it may make. They are meant to stay still, but how is something so light and weightless supposed to stay tied down in one spot? Watch _Twisted Joy_ to find out the confusion it ultimately brings.
