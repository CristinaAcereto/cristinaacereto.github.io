Artist Statement

The muse in my art is the one that allows a conversation upon the ideas of happiness and meaning in life. Life needs a full range of emotions to attain this valuable meaning and my art displays this with a full range of mediums - both traditional and digital. This range includes video art, installation and spatial design.

I am interested in the emotions of others and what pillars in life run human emotion; as well as how my art can send a message to allow others to understand these pillars as well. By using my own life experiences and those of others, we can all hopefully find fulfillment and true meaning in life.
