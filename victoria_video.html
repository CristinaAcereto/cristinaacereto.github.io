The Sad Trilogy of Unfamiliarity - Chapter 1: October 23rd

The Sad Trilogy of Unfamiliarity is an experimental video that allows insight into the passing of a loved one. It not only explains the thought of who is gone, but visually describes what the recipient emotes. Bringing together all aspects of what must be felt during a time like this, this video allows the time and space to grasp their feelings as well as yours. Watch Chapter 1 to tap into your most sorrowful thoughts.
